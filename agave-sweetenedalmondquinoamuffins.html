Almond Quinoa

Ingredients:

1 cup almond milk

1 Tblsp. ground flax seeds

1/4 cup canola oil

1/4 cup unsweetened Apple Sauce

1/2 tsp. vanilla extract

1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour (you can use whole wheat pastry flour, your muffin will just be a bit heavier)

1/4 cup almond meal

2 tsp. baking powder

1/2 tsp. salt

1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon

1/2 tsp. ground cardamom

1 1/4 cups cooked quinoa, cooled

1/2 cup finely chopped dried cherries (or whatever dried fruit & nuts you want to use)

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 Degrees F, and line or grease a 12-cup muffin tin.
  2. Whisk the almond milk and flax seeds in a small bowl.
  3. Sift the dry ingredients together into a big bowl.
  4. To the almond milk mixture, add the oil, agave, and vanilla.
  5. Pour the wet stuff into the dry stuff and mix with a wooden spoon just until it's combined.
  6. Add the quinoa and cherries and fold in gently to combine.
  7. Spoon the batter in the muffin tin, and bake for 20 minutes. Muffins are done when a toothpick inserted into the middle comes out clean.
