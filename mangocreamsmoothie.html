Mango Cream

Smoothie

Prep time:

10

mins

Total time:

10

mins

Ingredients:

1 cup unsweetened almond Milk

2 cups frozen mango chunks

2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice

20 seedless grapes

6 ice cubes

Directions:

Add all ingredients to the jug of your blender in the order they are listed and blend for 30 seconds or until smooth.
