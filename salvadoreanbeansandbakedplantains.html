Salvadorean Beans

and

Baked Plantains

Prep time:

15

mins

Cook Time:

40

mins

Total time:

55

mins

Ingredients:

3 cups or more cooked pinto beans

1/2 sliced onion

1/2 bell pepper, diced

2 tablespoons grape seed oil

salt and pepper

3 ripe plantains (they should be brown on the outside)

2 tablespoons grape seed oil

Directions:

Beans:

  1. Heat the oil and saut√© the onions and bell pepper on medium heat until they start to brown and caramelize. You will cook the beans in the same pan.
  2. Blend the onions and bell pepper with the beans until smooth. Turn off the heat to prevent the oil and pan from burning.
  3. Pour back in the same pan and cook for about 10 minutes on medium-low heat.

Plantains:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425.
  2. Slice the plantains diagonally into 1/4 inch slices and brush with oil.
  3. Place them on a baking sheet and bake for 30 minutes.
