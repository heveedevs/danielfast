Prayer

Daniel 10:2-3. 2.At that time I, Daniel, mourned for three weeks. 3. I ate no choice food; no meat or wine touched my lips; and I used no lotions at all until the three weeks were over.

In the old testament there are two passages that describe the fasts of Daniel. Combining those two brief descriptions, the Daniel Fast is now commonly used to refere to a fast that only includes vegetables and fruits to eat and water to drink. Dairy, meats, breads, and processed foods are avoided completely.

Prayer will see you through this time. God has done so much from you. He has brought you so far. A 21 day fast will be nothing compared to all the blessings you will receive.
