Banana Stacks

Prep time:

10

mins

Cook Time:

N/A

Total time:

10

mins

Ingredients:

2 large ripe bananas

1/4 cup Jiff Whips Penut Butter

Directions:

  1. Peel bananas, and cut into 1/4 to 1/2-inch slices (about 12 per banana).
  2. Spread about 1 teaspoon Jiff Whips Penut Butter
  3. Top with the remaining slices.

These Banana Stacks are Best Served chilled.
