Skinny Pear

Crisp

Prep time:

15

mins

Cook Time:

20-25

mins

Total time:

45-50

mins

Ingredients:

3 ripe pears

2 very ripe bananas (the more brown the better!)

1 Cup of Oats

1T grated ginger OR 1/2t ground ginger (Use fresh ginger if you can.)

1t ground cinnamon

Directions:

  1. First, preheat your oven to 350.
  2. Peel the pears and slice them very thin – but thicker than paper thin.
  3. Lightly spray an 8×8 or 9×9 dish with oil and line the pear slices inside.
  4. Atop the pears, grate about a thumb sized nub of fresh ginger root. (If you’re using ground ginger, skip the grating step and save the ground ginger for later.
  5. Next, mash the banana, cinnamon, ground ginger if using, and oats together with a fork or potato masher. Add the oats in 1/2 a cup at a time to make the mashing easier.
  6. Make sure all the oats are covered so they don’t dry out.
  7. Place this mixture atop the pears.
  8. spread it atop evenly,
  9. Bake at 350 for 20-25 minutes.
