Fresh Peach

Smoothie

Prep time:

5

mins

Total time:

5

mins

Ingredients:

  • 2 fresh peaches, peeled
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 cup ice

Directions:

Add all ingredients to the jug of your blender in the order they are listed and blend for 20-30 seconds, until smooth.
