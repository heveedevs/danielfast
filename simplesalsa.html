Simple Salsa

Prep time:

5

mins

Total time:

5

mins

Ingredients:

1 jalapeno or 3 dried chiles de arbol (use 1/2 bell pepper if you don't like spicy foods)

1/2 onion (optional)

cilantro (optional)

water

salt

Directions:

  1. Boil all the ingredients in a sauce pan for 5 minutes.
  2. Blend the tomato, jalapeno, and onion. Season with a little salt
  3. That's it!
