Hearty Hash

Browns

Prep time:

10

mins

Cook Time:

15-20

mins

Total time:

25-30

mins

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 2 Tbsp of extra virgin oil
  • 2 large potatoes, grated
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:

  1. Saute the onion and garlic in the oil until the onions are soft.
  2. Add the potatoes to the onion and garlic, and cook until tender.
  3. Add the salt and pepper, to taste.
