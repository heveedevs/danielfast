Workout Tips

Exercise is allowed during the fast and is highly recommended by the author of "The Daniel Fast," Susan Gregory. Most people experience an improvement in their health as a benefit of the Daniel fast, exercise is recommended during and as a continual part of health maintenance. Here are some other tips:

  • It is a good idea to keep your workouts a little shorter during this time. Most people experience fatigue and this is common.
  • Include nuts, nut butter, soy-based foods, legumes and green leafy vegetables for the protein you will need.
  • Drink plenty of pure water before, during and after exercise.
