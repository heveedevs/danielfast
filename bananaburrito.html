10

mins

10

mins

Ingredients:

Natural nut butter

Whole wheat tortilla's

diced banana

strawberries

raspberries

raw oats

chia seeds

chopped nuts

Directions:

Spread natural nut butter all over a whole wheat tortilla and fill with your choice of diced banana, strawberries, raspberries, raw oats, chia seeds, and/or chopped nuts.

enjoy!
